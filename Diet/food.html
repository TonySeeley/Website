Food

If you look at the meal tracking you will see that over a period of time I added more and more foods to my diet.

The logic behind this is that you start with nothing, then slowly add new foods while watching what affect the new food has on your progress/weigh loss.

The idea being we are all different and different foods affect us all in different ways.

Throughout the program you are encouraged to eat 5 times a day. This should include breakfast, a morning snack, lunch, an afternoon snack and dinner.

I kept this up for quite a few weeks but found it suited me personally better, to move my morning snack to early evening (after dinner) as this is when I wanted to pick at foods. This was OK'd by my dietitian so that's what I did.

Eating regular meals serves two purposes.
  1. Firstly, it keeps hunger at bay. If you always have some food inside you, you're not hungry. Also by not leaving a long time between meals, you don't develop that "I'm starving" feeling and eat everything in sight.

  2. Secondly, regular meals keep your metabolism going (and it's an active metabolism that burns fat). The analogy used to explain this to me was your body is like a fire, you have to keep feeding it to burn, let it die down and it takes a lot more wood to get it going again.
