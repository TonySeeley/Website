Ski Ready

Tuesday 25th November 2003

OK, this was a bit stupid but we thought it was funny :-)

We went out shopping and brought all our ski gear ready for the season.

When we got home we decided to try it all on and take some pictures.

It was Lisa's idea on the poses.

The idea was to buy bibs for Lisa and I (bibs are the waterproof trousers) however we went to REI - big mistake.

REI (Recreation Equipment Incorporated) is the North West's number one outdoor sporting store. They sell everything you need for hiking, biking, camping, climbing, skiing etc. etc.

We got into the shop and got completely carried away.

We brought ski boots, hats, gloves, coats, bibs, underwear, even blankets for the car - like I said big mistake.

Anyway when we got home we had a fashion show and the pictures were taken.

Although Abi wasn't wearing any ski clothes, she didn't want to be left out, so we took some pictures of her too.

Now all we need is snow.
