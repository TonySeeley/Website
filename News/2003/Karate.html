James first Karate stripe

Thursday 7th August 2003

After two months of intensive Karate lessons, James finally got a closer to his black belt.

Only a little closer mind you, as he now has one purple stripe on his white belt. (Oh yeah and he thinks he can beat me up - which is rubbish coz I can still take him - just).

In the pictures you can see James in his Gi (or white pyjamas to you and me), with the rest of his class.

His Sensei (Karate Teacher) must have the patience of a saint, as when I arrived I just got to see one little boy kick him in the back when he wasn't looking.

As in real life, the child (not mine thank god - he clearly realises the Sensei is even harder than me!) was sent to stand in the corner. (Very useful I think in a pub fight).

James now knows three kata's (Karate set pieces) that he will only do if bribed.

So if you ever see him do them will be amazed.

The Sensei tells the class every lesson that they must NOT perform Karate on their brothers and sisters, parents (very important that one) and anyone else they know, unless they are a "Bad Guy". (I still haven't ascertained how you become a "Bad Guy", but as soon as I find out I will post it here)
