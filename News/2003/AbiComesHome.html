Abi Comes Home

Friday 9th May 2003

Today at 11:05am Lisa and Abi came home.

Abi had not been feeding so the doctors decided to keep her in an extra day. However, she was fine last night so she came home (escaped from hospital) this morning.

Abi kept dropping her temperature as well, so they kept placing her under a heat lamp. All this because she wouldn't feed.

James was getting upset too, he told me last night that "He couldn't be a proper son without mummy home"!

I telephoned Lisa this morning and she told me Abi had cried all though the night, but did take from the bottle.

As a result she was allowed home today.
