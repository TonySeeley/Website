Baby Abi Arrives

Wednesday 7th May 2003

Today at 7:18pm Lisa gave birth to Abi Jayne Seeley, weighing in at 8 lbs after a 11 hour labour.

Mother and baby (along with father and son) are all doing fine :-)

Lisa had been having a lot of discomfort over the last few days (last 2 weeks really) and it was decided last week to start things moving.

As a result last Wednesday (30th April) Lisa visited the hospital to receive some "Lets get the pregnancy over" treatment.

Result: No Baby.

So Friday morning (2nd May) we went back again.

Result: No Baby.

Finally the hospital decided to admit Lisa to be induced on Sunday night/Monday morning at 12:00am.

We all got excited and expected Abi early Monday morning.

The hospital however phoned us back and said, "Let's make it 7am instead" (clearly Lisa's OB/GYN didn't want to get up that early).

So Monday morning (5th May) at 6am (after no sleep the night before) Lisa and I got up and telephoned the hospital, to be told that they were a bit busy and could we phone back later.

So a just after 7am Lisa phoned again.

"Sorry", we were told, "But 6 expectant mothers had just come in"

!!! THIS BABY WAS NEVER COMING OUT !!!

The hospital this time said they would call us.

So we waited... and waited... and waited...

Finally, we got a call at 1:30pm (on Monday) and were told we could go in... some time Tuesday, probably midnight!!!

So we waited all day Tuesday for a call, but nothing happened. So we called them. Again we were told probably midnight as they were again very busy.

At 11:00pm yesterday night (Tuesday) we called the hospital again, only to be told they were too busy again!

Lisa was really upset by this, she had anticipated going in and felt let down again.

They told us they would phone in the next couple of hours when a bed became free.

We woke up this morning at 7am, still no call from the hospital, so I called them. I was ready for a big argument too, about how emotional stress was driving Lisa mad (she hadn't slept in 3 days), but they just said come in now!

30 minutes later we were in the hospital and they started a drip to induce labour.

I won't go through all the messy details, but 11 hours later Abi arrived.

Lisa was fantastic, I am so proud of her. (I'm so glad I'm a man!!)

I went to fetch James and Lisa's mum (who had been looking after James all day) and James met his little sister for the first time.

The time is now 10:45pm PST and I'm exhausted, god only knows how Lisa feels. Lisa and Abi are staying in the hospital and come home tomorrow.

I have to say I can't wait.

I took some pictures and have posted then here; I will take more tomorrow and maybe even make a short film. So come back if you want to see more.

So in true female Seeley fashion Abi kept us all waiting :-)
