Redmond Snow

January 2004

When we moved to the US everyone told us "it never snows in Redmond". Talk about famous last words!

Every year since we have been here we have had snow, sometimes a little and sometimes WAY TO MUCH. For those that don't know the landscape here in Washington State is very hilly in places, so snow is not that welcome. Oh yeah the kids love it, but driving is just not possible.

When the snow arrives a number of roads in downtown Seattle are closed and loads of people couldn't get into work.

If you are unlucky enough to at work when the snow comes, you just can't get home! One year it took me 4 hours to drive 8 miles and along the way were numerous cars that had been left at the road side by people who couldn't handle the slippery conditions.

I remember the following morning fighting for an hour to get back into work, only to find that no one else bothered. So I just drove back home.

Anyway, every year when it snows I take some pictures. I may not like driving in it but it's really beautiful. The pics below were taken in January 2004.
