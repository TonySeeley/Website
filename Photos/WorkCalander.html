Team Calendar

May 1999

Back in 1999 I was the development director for a software company called PWA. The people I worked with were amazing, we had a wonderful 7 years building a company up from 19 people to 180.

Anyway every couple of months we used to have a company meeting and each team used to present to the rest of the company. In development we were getting very board with the same old presentations from everyone each meeting and wanted to shake things up a bit.

At around the same time, the company had just commissioned a calendar to send out to customers that included some odd slogans that we were assured would resonate with our customers. We decided in development to do a parody of the calendar and incorporate the pictures into our company meeting presentation.

To help people get it we introduced each picture (modeled by one of the team) next to the original. Everyone fell about laughing, it was a huge success. Perhaps the most ironic surprise was the marketing company who made the original happened to attend the meeting. They too loved the images and wanted to do another version, but the management got scared and it never happened.

I remember we had a great time making this and when every I look at the pictures I can't help but smile - memories of happy times with a really great team.
