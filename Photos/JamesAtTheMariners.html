James Announcing at the Mariners

April 2004

Lisa's sister Nikki and her two boys were visiting so we decided to get some tickets to a Mariners game.

When Lisa brought the tickets she entered James into a kids competition for the game we were going to see. Amazingly James was picked and given the job of being a guest PA Announcer.

Now for those who don't know James well I should point out that he is quite shy, so the thought of him announcing to 40 thousand spectators who was about to bat was laughable. In fact at first he didn't want to do it. Then we pointed out he would get some "freebies" and after much enthusiasm from his friends he changed his mind.

At the game I have NEVER been more nervous. He was, I have to say, FANTASTIC. He read the batting orders out clearly and sounded great. I was so proud of him I can't begin to tell you.
